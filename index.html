A travel website is an online platform designed to help users plan, book, and manage
their travel experiences. These websites typically offer a range of services,
including flight bookings, hotel reservations, car rentals, and vacation packages.
Users can search for travel options based on their desired destination, dates, and
budget, and compare prices from different providers.
